About

Stephen Cramer has electrified the Boston area music scene with inspired live lighting design, visual effects, and atmospherics since 2011. His unparalleled musicality and dynamic live productions provide both artists and audiences with a unique and unforgettable concert experience.

Stephen is currently based out of Boston’s historic Paradise Rock Club as the venue’s lead Lighting Designer. In addition to live concert performances, Stephen also provides bespoke lighting design and installation services to the area’s hottest nightclubs and bars, as well as spinning lights to live DJ sets. An alumnus of Emerson College, Stephen earned his Bachelor of the Arts degree in Video Production in 2008.

For booking and consultation information, please contact stephenccramer@gmail.com.
