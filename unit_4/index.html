About

Stephen Cramer has electrified the Boston area music scene with inspired live lighting design, visual effects, and atmospherics since 2011. His unparalleled musicality and dynamic live lighting production provides both artist and audience with a unique and unforgettable concert experience.

From hip-hop to hardcore, psychedelic funk to ethereal post-rock, Stephen’s work elevates artists of any genre. Past performances include: Big Boi, Lizzo, Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, DJ Questlove, Eddie Vedder, Brazilian Girls, Lil Xan, Guster, Fidlar, Men Without Hats, Nada Surf, The Psychadelic Furs, and many, many more.

Stephen is currently based at Boston’s historic Paradise Rock Club where he serves as the venue’s lead Lighting Designer. In addition to live concert performances, Stephen also provides bespoke lighting design and installation services to the area’s hottest nightclubs and bars, as well as spinning lights to live DJ sets. An alumnus of Emerson College, Stephen earned his Bachelor of the Arts degree in Video Production in 2008.

For booking and consultation information, please contact stephenccramer@gmail.com.
